Living well begins
with eating well

The Honey Company offers limited quantities of our honeybees, beekeeping equipment, honey, and specialty products at specific times of the year. In addition, we are developing a series of educational resources to help you keep stronger, healthier honeybee colonies.

Our honey is freshly extracted throughout each season and bloom or nectar flow, with the exception of winter where we are thankful for the leftover spring and summer harvest and anxiously await the spring again. Most of our wildflower honey contains Orange Blossom, Saw Palmetto, Mangrove, Brazillian Pepper, and other native plants that are in abundanced.
